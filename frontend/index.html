Help & FAQ

Login or Google Sign-In isn't working?
If you are having trouble signing in, signing up, or the Google login window isn't appearing, your device's security settings might be blocking it. To fix this:

1. Long-press the Vaad app icon on your home screen and select App Info (or the 'i' icon).
2. Tap on Site Settings.
3. Ensure the following permissions are set to Allow:
  • Third-party sign-in (Allows Google Login)
  • Pop-ups and redirects (Allows login windows)
  • Notifications & Sound (For future case alerts)
Tip: You can always use the standard Email & Password form to register or log in securely without changing any device settings.
How do I track a court case?
Go to the main search screen. You can search using the 16-digit CNR Number, or by Advocate Name, Party Name, and Judge Name. If you have a Pro plan, you can use the Bulk CNR tab to refresh up to 50 cases at once.
What is the Practice Dashboard?
The Practice Dashboard is your personal ledger. It allows you to track the total expected fees for a case, set per-hearing installments, and log payments. The dashboard will automatically calculate your pending dues and total revenue.
How do I add a case to my Dashboard?
Method 1: Search for a case on the home screen. In the results, click the "💼 Add to My Dashboard" button to auto-fill the details.

Method 2: Open your Dashboard from the menu and click the "+ Add New Case" button to manually enter a client's details, even if the case isn't filed yet.
What is Universal Search?
Universal Search helps you find your saved dashboard records instantly. Just type a client's name, a CNR number, or even a fee amount, and it will pull up the specific case from your ledger so you can log a payment immediately.
How do the FUP (Search Limits) work?
To prevent abuse of the official API, searches are limited. Free users get 1 trial search. You can upgrade to a Pro (30 searches), Pro Max (100 searches), or Supreme (150 searches) plan. Your limits reset automatically every 30 days based on your billing cycle.
