Help & FAQ

How do I search for a case?

Go to the main search screen. Enter the 16-character alphanumeric CNR number found on court documents into the CNR input field. Alternatively, you can search by Petitioner/Respondent Name.

What do I do if my search limit is reached?

Free users get 1 trial search. Signing in with Google unlocks 5 daily free searches. If you need more, you can upgrade via UPI for 100 monthly searches.
