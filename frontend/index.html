Help & FAQ

Login or Google Sign-In isn't working?
If you are having trouble signing in, your device's security settings might be blocking it. To fix this:

1. Long-press the Vaad app icon on your home screen and select App Info.
2. Tap on Site Settings.
3. Ensure the following are set to Allow:
  • Third-party sign-in (Allows Google Login)
  • Pop-ups and redirects (Allows login windows)
Tip: You can always use the standard Email & Password form to log in securely without changing device settings.
How do I track a court case?
Go to the main search screen. You can search using the 16-digit CNR Number, or by Advocate Name, Party Name, and Judge Name. If you have a Pro Max plan, you can use the Bulk CNR tab to refresh up to 50 cases at once.
What is the Practice Dashboard?
The Practice Dashboard is your personal ledger. It allows you to track the total expected fees for a case, set per-hearing installments, and log payments. The dashboard will automatically calculate your pending dues and total revenue.
How do the FUP (Search Limits) work?
To prevent abuse of the official API, searches and PDF downloads are limited based on your tier. Free users get 1 trial search and 5 AI queries. You can upgrade to a Pro, Pro Max, or Supreme plan. Your limits reset automatically every 30 days based on your billing cycle.
