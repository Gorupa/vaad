Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Digital Goods & Instant Allocation

All payments made to Raghav Communication for Vaad premium plans (Pro, Pro Max, Supreme) are processed instantly via our secure payment gateway partner, Razorpay. Because digital goods, search quotas, and PDF download capabilities are allocated to your account immediately upon a successful transaction, we generally do not offer refunds once a plan is activated.

2. Cancellations

Vaad operates on a 30-day non-recurring cycle. We do not automatically charge your card or UPI account at the end of the 30 days. You must manually initiate a new payment to renew your plan. Therefore, formal "cancellations" of active subscriptions are not required. If you choose not to renew, your account will simply revert to the Free tier at the end of your billing cycle.

3. Failed Transactions & Double Charges

If your payment fails or money is deducted from your bank account but your Vaad account is not upgraded, the amount will automatically be refunded by Razorpay to your original payment method. This process typically takes 5-7 business days depending on your bank.

If you experience a double charge for a single upgrade, please contact our support team immediately with your Razorpay transaction IDs, and we will initiate a manual refund for the duplicate transaction.
