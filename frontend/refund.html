Refund & Cancellation

Last updated: March 2026

1. Digital Product Policy

Vaad is a digital Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform. Because your subscription immediately grants you access to API credits (searches, PDF downloads, and AI processing power) which incur hard costs on our end, all sales are final and non-refundable once a payment is successfully captured.

2. Cancellations

We operate on a strict "No Auto-Billing" policy. We do not save your payment methods for automatic monthly deductions. Your subscription will naturally expire 30 days from the date of purchase. To cancel, simply do not initiate a renewal payment.

3. Failed Transactions

If your money is deducted by your bank or UPI app but your Vaad account is not upgraded, the transaction is considered failed by the payment gateway. In such cases, Razorpay will automatically process a refund to your original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.

4. Upgrading Plans

If you choose to upgrade your plan (e.g., moving from Pro Max to Supreme) before your 30-day cycle is over, the new plan will take effect immediately, and a new 30-day cycle will begin. Previous balances or unused searches do not roll over and are not prorated.
