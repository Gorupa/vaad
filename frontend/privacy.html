Privacy Policy

"Vaad" is a digital product and brand owned, operated, and managed by Raghav Communication.

1. Information We Collect

When you log in to Vaad using Google Authentication, we collect your basic public profile information, specifically your name, email address, and profile picture. We use this strictly to create your account and manage your subscription tier. If you use the Practice Dashboard, we collect the case titles, CNR numbers, and fee data you choose to input.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide you with the Vaad service, track your search limits according to your active plan, and securely link your subscription to your identity. Search queries (CNRs, Advocate names, etc.) are passed securely to the eCourtsIndia API to retrieve your results. We do not manually log your public case search history on our servers. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.

3. Third-Party Services & Payment Data

For processing subscriptions, we use Razorpay as our secure payment gateway partner. We do not store your highly sensitive financial data (like full card numbers or UPI PINs) on our servers. Razorpay processes this data securely. We only store your payment status, transaction ID, and billing history to maintain your Vaad subscription. We also use Firebase (Google) for secure user authentication and database syncing.

4. Public Court Data

The case information, histories, and orders displayed on Vaad are public records sourced from third-party APIs. Vaad does not generate, alter, or claim ownership of this public legal data.

5. Data Storage & Local Caching (PWA)

To ensure compliance and maximum security, all user profiles and Practice Dashboard ledger data are stored on localized servers located in Mumbai, India (asia-south1).

To provide a lightning-fast experience, Vaad operates as a Progressive Web App (PWA). We use standard web technologies to save the app's structural files directly on your device. We do not cache your live court case data or personal authentication details offline. You can clear this cached data at any time through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights (DPDP Act, 2023) & Contact

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to notice, consent, and erasure. You can delete specific case data directly from your dashboard, or permanently erase your entire account using the tool below.
