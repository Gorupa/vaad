Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Vaad, we collect basic authentication data via Google Sign-In (Name, Email, Profile Picture) to create and manage your account. If you use the Practice Dashboard, we collect the case titles, CNR numbers, and fee data you choose to input.

2. Search Queries & Data Processing

Search queries (CNRs, Advocate names, etc.) are passed securely to the eCourtsIndia API to retrieve your results. We do not manually log your public case search history on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Used for secure user authentication, database syncing, and tracking subscription cycles.
  • Razorpay: Handles all payment processing. We do not see, collect, or store your credit card or UPI details.
  • eCourtsIndia API: Provides the official public court data.

4. Data Storage & Localization

To ensure compliance and maximum security, all user profiles and Practice Dashboard ledger data are stored on localized servers located in Mumbai, India (asia-south1).

5. Data Sharing

Raghav Communication does not sell, rent, or trade your personal or client information to any external agencies, data brokers, or marketing firms under any circumstances.

6. Your Rights (DPDP Act, 2023 Compliance)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Notice & Consent: We will explicitly ask for your consent before syncing your Practice Dashboard data to the cloud.
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete specific case data directly from your dashboard, or permanently erase your entire account and all associated data using the account deletion tool below.
  • Grievance Redressal: If you have concerns regarding your data privacy, you may contact our Grievance Officer at Raghav Communication, Udaipur, Rajasthan. Support Email: gauravkalal1719@gmail.com.
