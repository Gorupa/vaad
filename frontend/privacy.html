Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Vaad, we collect basic authentication data via Google Sign-In (Name, Email, Profile Picture) to create and manage your account. We also track your subscription status and Fair Usage Policy (FUP) counts.

2. Search Queries & Data Processing

Search queries (CNRs, Advocate names, etc.) are passed securely to the eCourtsIndia API to retrieve your results. We do not store your personal case search history on our servers. Your data requests are processed in real-time.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Used for secure user authentication and tracking subscription cycles.
  • Razorpay: Handles all payment processing. We do not see, collect, or store your credit card or UPI details.
  • eCourtsIndia API: Provides the official public court data and AI summaries.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your FUP usage metrics are stored securely in your browser's LocalStorage and our Firebase database. We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL/TLS) to protect your data during transmission.

5. Data Sharing

Raghav Communication does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any external agencies or marketing firms.
