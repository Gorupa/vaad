Privacy Policy

"Vaad" is a digital product and brand owned, operated, and managed by Raghav Communication.

1. Information We Collect

When you log in to Vaad using Google Authentication, we collect your basic public profile information, specifically your name, email address, and profile picture. We use this strictly to create your account and manage your subscription tier.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide you with the Vaad service, track your search limits according to your active plan, and securely link your subscription to your identity. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.

3. Payment Processing Data

For processing subscriptions, we use Razorpay as our secure payment gateway partner. We do not store your highly sensitive financial data (like full card numbers or UPI PINs) on our servers. Razorpay processes this data securely. We only store your payment status, transaction ID, and billing history to maintain your Vaad subscription. The data collected is managed by Raghav Communication.

4. Public Court Data

The case information, histories, and orders displayed on Vaad are public records sourced from third-party APIs. Vaad does not generate, alter, or claim ownership of this public legal data.

5. Local Storage & Caching (PWA)

To provide a lightning-fast experience, Vaad operates as a Progressive Web App (PWA). We use standard web technologies, including Service Workers, Browser Cache, and Local Storage, to save the app's structural files (HTML, CSS) directly on your device. This allows the app to open instantly. We do not cache your live court case data or personal authentication details offline. You can clear this cached data at any time through your browser or device app settings.
